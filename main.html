About US

Welcome to our Clash of Clans progress tracker! Our platform is designed to help you accurately measure and track your progress in Clash of Clans, ensuring you know exactly how much more effort is needed to max out your Town Hall base.

We utilize a special algorithm that meticulously calculates your progress, taking into account all the upgrades and resources you have collected. This sophisticated algorithm provides a precise estimation of what is left to upgrade, allowing you to strategize and optimize your gameplay effectively.

Our goal is to help you achieve your Clash of Clans milestones with ease and efficiency. Join our community of dedicated players and take your game to the next level!
